# Calling All Adventurers


## Mechanics
- Buildings can be destroyed by monsters
    -
- 5 stats
    - MGC (damage from spells)
    - ATK (damage from normal attacks)
    - DEF (fixed damage reduction)
    - HP  (health)
    - SPD (the fast)
- Killing monsters gives EXP
    - Level cap is 9
    - Gain a new ability every 3 levels
    - Can upgrade a stat
